Frequently Asked Questions

   

Everything you need to know about our mission and programs.

   
     
               
SIPE's main goal is to create a standardized, nationwide framework for fostering innovation and entrepreneurship within educational institutions, bridging the gap between academia and industry.
     
     
               
Our program is designed for educational institutions. Once an institution partners with us, all its students become eligible to participate in our programs, workshops, and mentorship opportunities.
     
     
               
We provide a cross-college Mentor Connect Platform that links students with experienced industry professionals, researchers, and entrepreneurs for personalized guidance and project support.
     
     
               
Please visit our registration portal and sign up as an institution. Our outreach team will get in touch with you to discuss the partnership model and next steps.
