Frequently Asked Questions

What is the primary goal of SIPE?

SIPE's main goal is to create a standardized, nationwide framework for fostering innovation and entrepreneurship within educational institutions. We aim to bridge the gap between academia and industry, helping students turn their ideas into impactful ventures.

Who is eligible to join the SIPE network?

Our program is designed for educational institutions (universities, colleges, technical institutes) across India. Once an institution becomes a partner, all of its students are eligible to participate in our programs and access our network.

How does the mentorship program work?

We provide a cross-college Mentor Connect Platform that links students with experienced industry professionals, researchers, and successful entrepreneurs. Mentorship is provided through one-on-one guidance, group sessions, and specialized bootcamps.

How can our institution partner with SIPE?

We are always looking for new institutional partners. Please visit our registration portal and sign up as an institution. Our outreach team will review your application and get in touch to discuss the partnership process.

Have More Questions?

Our team is ready to help. Reach out to us for partnership inquiries, program details, or any other questions you may have.
